### What if I don't have technical staff?
No problem! The eflury Method includes full training. Your team just needs to be willing to learn. Most clients have zero technical staff.

Yes! We specialize in Swiss business software: bexio, ABACUS, SAP, Sage, run.my, Odoo, custom systems, and more.

Absolutely. We follow Swiss data protection laws (FADP), can host everything in Switzerland (Exoscale, Infomaniak), and sign comprehensive data processing agreements.

We include refinement cycles in all packages. If it's not working, we fix it. Enterprise clients have dedicated success managers.
