Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need technical skills?

None. You command the AI in plain language via WhatsApp or your preferred messenger. We handle all technical setup.

Is my data safe?

The Mac Mini runs in your office, on your network. With the Enterprise plan, no data ever leaves your premises. Full Swiss data protection.

What are the ongoing costs?

The monthly fee includes Claude AI access. No surprise API costs. Enterprise plan with self-hosted models has no mandatory monthly fees.

What can it actually do?

Email triage, calendar management, file organization, research, content drafting, expense tracking, and custom automations we build for you.

How fast does it respond?

Typically 5-15 seconds for most tasks. The Mac Mini runs 24/7 and responds even when your main computer is off.

Can I return it?

14-day satisfaction guarantee. If it's not right for your business, return the hardware for a full refund (minus training time).
