Common Questions

How do you work with SMEs outside Grenchen?

I work remotely with clients across Switzerland and beyond. Most of my work is done through video calls, screen sharing, and secure file exchange. I can visit your office for kickoff meetings and key milestones — but the actual automation work happens digitally.

What's the typical project duration?

Most projects take 4-12 weeks depending on complexity. A simple Claude Skill might take 2-3 weeks, while a full finance automation system could be 8-12 weeks. I'll give you a precise timeline after our discovery call.

Do I need technical expertise on my team?

No. I design solutions for non-technical users. Your team will use simple interfaces — they won't need to code or understand AI. I handle all the technical complexity so you can focus on your business.

How do you handle data security and privacy?

All solutions are FADP (Swiss Data Privacy) compliant. Data stays in Switzerland where possible, and I use enterprise-grade encryption. For sensitive industries, I can implement additional security measures like air-gapped systems or on-premise deployment.

What's the ROI timeline?

Most clients see positive ROI within 3-6 months. My own firm achieved payback in 3.2 months. I provide detailed ROI projections during the discovery phase so you know exactly what to expect.

What happens after implementation?

Every package includes training for your team. For ongoing support, I offer maintenance packages that include updates, refinements, and priority support. Your automation keeps working even as your business evolves.
