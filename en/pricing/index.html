Pricing FAQs

Why upfront pricing instead of "Contact us"?

Because Swiss SMEs value transparency. Hidden pricing = wasted time. I show you exactly what it costs so you can decide faster.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes! Professional and Enterprise packages offer split payments (50% upfront, 50% at deployment). Starter is one payment.

What's included in the "Claude Team subscription"?

Claude Team costs CHF 280/month (3 users). I include it for the duration specified in each package, so you don't pay separately during implementation.

What if I need fewer than 2 Skills?

For single-Skill projects, I offer a CHF 5,900 Micro package (2 weeks, 1 Skill, 1 MCP integration). Contact me to discuss.

Do you charge hourly after the package ends?

No. Post-launch support is included (2-8 weeks depending on package). After that, you can add Extended Support (CHF 1,200/month) or pay per-project for new Skills.

What's your refund policy?

Professional and Enterprise only: If you don't achieve ≥15 hours/week time savings within 3 months of deployment, full refund. Starter packages are non-refundable (too short to measure ROI).

Are there any hidden fees?

No. The only additional cost is 8.1% VAT (Swiss law). Everything else – hosting, software licenses during development, training materials – is included.

Can I upgrade from Starter to Professional later?

Yes! If you start with Starter and want to expand, I'll credit your CHF 9,900 toward Professional (pay CHF 15,000 difference). Must upgrade within 6 months.

Do you offer discounts?

Occasionally, yes. I run seasonal promotions (e.g., -10% in Q1 for early-stage startups). Subscribe to my newsletter to hear about them.

What if my project needs more than 10 Skills?

For >10 Skills, I create a custom Enterprise+ quote. Pricing scales: CHF 2,500/Skill for 11-20 Skills, CHF 2,000/Skill for 20+.
