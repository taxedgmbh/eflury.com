The eflury Method™

Big consultancies sell methodology as a sales slide. We publish ours: five phases, each with a clear goal, named deliverables, a decision gate — and what we need from you. No project enters a phase before the previous gate is passed.

  1. 1

    Discovery

    Week 1

    Understand where automation actually creates value — before anyone talks technology.

    • Process inventory with time-cost scoring
    • Prioritized automation roadmap
    • ROI projection per initiative
    Gate: A joint go/no-go decision based on the numbers — a "no-go" is a clean outcome too.
    Your contribution: 2–3 hours of interviews with the people affected, read access to the relevant systems.
    Available standalone: AI Audit — CHF 4'900, fully credited
  2. 2

    Design

    Week 2

    Design the solution completely before anything is built.

    • Solution blueprint (Skills, MCP connectors, data flows, review gates)
    • Written acceptance criteria
    • Security and data-protection concept per our published standards
    Gate: Your sign-off on the blueprint — development starts only after that.
    Your contribution: One review meeting, decisions on open design questions.
  3. 3

    Development

    Weeks 3–5

    Build and test with your real data — not demo data.

    • The Skills and connectors themselves
    • Test protocol against the phase-2 acceptance criteria
    • Technical documentation
    Gate: Acceptance test passed — measured against the pre-agreed criteria, not our impression.
    Your contribution: Test data, feedback in two short review rounds.
  4. 4

    Deployment

    Week 6

    Production operation with a team that understands the system.

    • Team training
    • Operations runbook (usage, edge cases, escalation)
    • Supervised go-live
    Gate: Productive operation without our daily involvement.
    Your contribution: Team time for the training (typically half a day).
  5. 5

    Optimization

    Ongoing

    Measure whether the promised results materialize — and refine.

    • Metrics report (time saved, error rates, usage)
    • Prioritized improvement backlog
    Your contribution: One short review per month.
    Available as a service: Managed AI Operations — from CHF 1'200/month
