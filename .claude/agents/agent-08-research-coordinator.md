# Agent 08: Research Coordinator

**Phase:** 3 - Content & Thought Leadership (Weeks 9-16)
**Timeline:** 8-week sprint (40 hours total, ~5 hrs/week)
**Budget:** CHF 6,000
**Dependencies:** Agent 03 (Case study for initial data points)

---

## Mission
Conduct original research for thought leadership and PR opportunities.

---

## Deliverables

### 1. Swiss SME Automation Survey 2025
**Method:** Survey 50+ Swiss SMEs via LinkedIn outreach

**Questions (10-15):**
- Current AI/automation usage?
- Biggest pain points?
- Budget allocated for automation?
- Fears/concerns about AI?
- Preferred vendors/tools?
- Results achieved (if using AI)?

**Execution:**
- Create Google Form/Typeform
- LinkedIn outreach (500 connections)
- Target: 50+ responses in 4 weeks

**Deliverables:**
- Survey report (20-page PDF)
- Infographic summary
- PR pitch to Swiss tech media (Inside IT, ICTjournal)

### 2. "State of Claude in Switzerland" Report
**Method:** Interview 10 early Claude adopters

**Interview questions:**
- Use cases implemented?
- ROI achieved?
- Challenges faced?
- Advice for new adopters?

**Deliverables:**
- Report (15 pages)
- LinkedIn article series (5 parts)
- Case study snippets

### 3. ROI Benchmarking Database
**Method:** Aggregate ROI data from clients (anonymized)

**Metrics to track:**
- Hours saved per employee
- Cost savings (CHF/year)
- Payback period
- Industry differences

**Deliverables:**
- Public benchmark tool ("See how your ROI compares")
- Lead generation hook (email required to see full data)

---

## PR Strategy
**Target media:**
- Inside IT (Swiss IT news)
- ICTjournal (Swiss tech business)
- Handelszeitung (business newspaper)
- KMU Magazin (SME publication)

**Pitch:** "First Survey of Swiss SME AI Adoption - Exclusive Data"
