## Mission
Build engaged community around Claude automation in Switzerland across LinkedIn, newsletter, GitHub, and YouTube.

---

## Deliverables

### 1. LinkedIn Strategy
**Frequency:** 3 posts/week (Monday/Wednesday/Friday)

**Content mix:**
- 40% Educational (tips, tutorials, "did you know")
- 30% Case studies & results (Taxed + client stories)
- 20% Thought leadership (opinions, predictions, Swiss market insights)
- 10% Personal (behind-the-scenes, Grenchen life, team moments)

**Posting schedule:**
- Monday: Educational (e.g., "5-minute Claude tip")
- Wednesday: Case study snippet or client win
- Friday: Thought leadership or personal story

**Engagement strategy:**
- Respond to all comments within 24 hours
- Engage with 10 other posts daily (comments, not just likes)
- Connect with 50+ Swiss SME decision-makers/week
- Tag partners, clients (with permission)

**Goal:** 2,000 followers by Week 24 (current: estimate ~500)

### 2. LinkedIn Group: "Claude Automation Switzerland"
**Purpose:** Community for Swiss Claude users/enthusiasts

**Content strategy:**
- Weekly discussion topics (e.g., "What process did you automate this week?")
- Share member wins (celebrate successes)
- Invite guest speakers (partners, clients, Anthropic team)
- Moderate for quality (no spam, keep focused)

**Growth strategy:**
- Invite all LinkedIn connections
- Promote in webinars (Agent 11)
- Partner cross-promotion (Agent 10)

**Goal:** 250+ members by Week 24

### 3. Newsletter: "Claude Automation Digest"
**Frequency:** Bi-weekly (every 2 weeks)
**Platform:** Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or EmailJS

**Content per issue:**
- 1 featured blog post (from Agent 07)
- 2-3 curated Claude news/updates
- 1 case study snippet
- 1 tip/tutorial
- 1 community spotlight (member success)
- Upcoming events (webinars, workshops)

**Growth strategy:**
- Signup form on website (footer, pop-up)
- Lead magnet incentive (Agent 06 resources)
- Webinar attendees auto-subscribed (opt-in)

**Goal:** 500+ subscribers by Week 24

**Goal:** 100+ stars by Week 24

### 5. YouTube Channel
**Channel name:** "Emanuel Flury - Claude Automation"
**Frequency:** Weekly videos (5-10 min)

**Content series:**
1. "Claude Tips" (weekly quick tutorials)
2. "Skills Development" (step-by-step coding)
3. "Client Success Stories" (interview format)
