# Agent 10: Partnership Outreach Strategist

**Phase:** 4 - Ecosystem & Partnerships (Weeks 17-24)
**Timeline:** 8-week sprint (30 hours total, ~4 hrs/week)
**Budget:** CHF 4,500
**Dependencies:** Agent 03 (Case study for partnership pitch proof)

---

## Mission
Build strategic partnerships with complementary service providers for referral leads.

---

## Target Partnerships (4 minimum)

### 1. Accounting Software Providers
**Targets:** Bexio, AbaNinja, Sage (Swiss SME accounting SaaS)

**Pitch:**
"Add Claude automation as value-add service for your 10,000+ clients.
We handle implementation, you earn 20% referral fee + happier customers."

**Value proposition:**
- Differentiation from competitors
- Sticky product (reduces churn)
- Additional revenue stream
- Co-marketing opportunities

**Deliverables:**
- Outreach email template
- Partnership deck (10 slides)
- Revenue share agreement template
- Joint webinar offer

### 2. Swiss IT Consulting Firms
**Targets:** Local IT consultants (non-competing, 5-20 employees)

**Pitch:**
"Offer Claude automation without building expertise.
We white-label, you maintain client relationship, 30% commission."

**Value proposition:**
- New service offering (no R&D cost)
- Training program for their staff
- Shared client success
- Co-branded case studies

**Deliverables:**
- White-label program terms
- Training curriculum (2-day workshop)
- Co-marketing materials

### 3. Anthropic Direct Partnership
**Target:** Apply for official Anthropic partner status

**Strategy:**
- Reach out via partner program application
- Pitch: "First Claude-exclusive consultant in Switzerland"
- Offer to co-create case studies
- Request early access to new features
- Goal: Featured on Anthropic customer stories page

**Deliverables:**
- Partner application submission
- Case study drafts for Anthropic review
- Request for partner badge/logo

### 4. Swiss Business Associations
**Targets:** KMU Verband, regional Handelskammern (chambers of commerce)

**Pitch:**
"Offer your members 10% discount on Claude automation.
We speak at your events, contribute to newsletters, sponsor relevant programs."

**Value proposition:**
- Member benefit (exclusive discount)
- Thought leadership (Emanuel speaks)
- Newsletter content (automation tips)
- Sponsor visibility

**Deliverables:**
- Association membership (if required)
- Speaking topics (3-4 talk titles)
- Newsletter article templates

---

## Execution Plan

**Weeks 17-18:** Research & outreach list
- Identify 20 potential partners (5 per category)
- Find contact info (LinkedIn, websites)
- Draft personalized outreach emails

**Weeks 19-20:** First outreach wave
- Send 20 emails (5/day)
- Follow-up after 3-5 days
- Track responses in CRM (Notion/Airtable)

**Weeks 21-22:** Negotiations & agreements
- Schedule calls with interested partners
- Present partnership deck
- Negotiate terms (commission %, co-marketing)
- Draft agreements

**Weeks 23-24:** Launch & co-marketing
- Sign first 2-4 partnerships
- Plan joint webinars
- Create co-branded materials
- Announce partnerships (LinkedIn, website)
