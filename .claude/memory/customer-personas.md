### Buying Triggers

2. **Lost Client Due to Slow Response:**

3. **Burnout Risk:**
   Missed kid's soccer game for 3rd week in a row, wife had "the talk"
   → Need to free up time

4. **Competitor Using AI:**
   Saw competitor post on LinkedIn: "How we automated our client onboarding with AI"
   → FOMO (fear of missing out)

**Long-Term Triggers (Planning):**
1. **Q4 Budget Planning:** October-November, reviewing next year's expenses
2. **New Year Resolutions:** January, "This year we finally automate"
3. **Business Coach Recommendation:** External advisor suggests automation

### Decision-Making Process

**Stage 1: Awareness (Month 1)**
- Searches: "KMU Automatisierung Schweiz", "AI für Beratung"
- Reads: Blog posts, LinkedIn articles, Swiss business media
- Asks: Peers in networking groups "Who's using AI?"

**Stage 2: Research (Month 2-3)**
- Visits: 5-8 consulting websites (eflury.com, competitors, generalists)
- Compares: Pricing (if transparent), methodology, case studies
- Downloads: White papers, playbooks, ROI calculators
- Watches: Webinars, YouTube explainers

**Stage 3: Evaluation (Month 3-4)**
- Narrows to: 3 finalists (based on recommendations + self-research)
- Requests: Proposals from 2-3 consultants
- Discusses: With CFO partner (Sandra persona), maybe CTO friend
- Checks: References, LinkedIn profiles, Google reviews

**Stage 4: Decision (Month 4-5)**
- Final criteria:
  1. **ROI clarity** - "Will this pay for itself in <6 months?"
  2. **Proven results** - "Has this worked for similar Swiss SMEs?"
  3. **Trustworthiness** - "Can I trust this person with our data/processes?"
  4. **Simplicity** - "Is this easy to implement without IT team?"
  5. **Swiss compliance** - "Is this FADP/GDPR compliant?"

**Stage 5: Purchase (Month 5)**
- Selects: Most likely Professional package (CHF 15K)
- Negotiates: Maybe asks for payment plan (3 installments)
- Signs: After showing proposal to finance partner

**Cycle Length:** 4-6 months from first awareness to signature
