## Content Production Workflow

### Writing Process
1. **Research:** 2 hours (keyword analysis, competitor content gap)
2. **Outline:** 1 hour (structure, key points, CTAs)
3. **First Draft:** 4-5 hours (for 2,500-word post)
4. **SEO Optimization:** 1 hour (keywords, meta, links)
5. **Images/Graphics:** 2 hours (Canva, screenshots, diagrams)
6. **Editing:** 1 hour (Grammarly, readability, fact-check)
7. **Publishing:** 30 minutes (WordPress/Astro, schema markup, social prep)

**Total per post:** ~12 hours

### Quality Standards
- **Readability:** Flesch reading ease 60+ (Grade 8-9 level)
- **Tone:** Professional but conversational (Swiss business style)
- **Length:** 2,000-3,000 words (depth over volume)
- **Sources:** Cite official sources (Anthropic docs, Swiss government, studies)
- **Visuals:** Minimum 3 custom images per post
- **CTAs:** 2-3 per post (mid-content + end)
