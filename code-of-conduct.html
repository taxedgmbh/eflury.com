Code of Conduct

Last updated: October 26, 2025

Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, Emanuel Flury is committed to making participation in our projects and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

  • Using welcoming and inclusive language
  • Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
  • Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
  • Focusing on what is best for the community and clients
  • Showing empathy towards other community members
  • Maintaining professional conduct in all interactions

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

  • The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
  • Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
  • Public or private harassment
  • Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
  • Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

Professional Conduct

As a professional consultant, Emanuel Flury adheres to the following principles:

  • Integrity: Honest and transparent communication with all stakeholders
  • Confidentiality: Protecting client information and intellectual property
  • Quality: Delivering high-quality work that meets or exceeds expectations
  • Compliance: Adhering to all applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards
  • Continuous Improvement: Staying current with industry best practices and technologies

Client Relationships

We are committed to:

  • Clear and timely communication
  • Realistic expectations and deliverables
  • Transparency in pricing and project scope
  • Respect for client time and resources
  • Professional boundaries and conflict of interest management

Data Protection & Privacy

We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of all data entrusted to us, in full compliance with Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP) and other applicable regulations. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Intellectual Property

We respect intellectual property rights and expect others to do the same. All work products are delivered in accordance with agreed-upon terms and ownership arrangements.

Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting us at me@eflury.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.

Swiss Professional Standards

Our conduct aligns with Swiss professional standards and business ethics, including compliance with:

  • Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP)
  • Swiss Code of Obligations
  • Professional standards for tax and financial advisors in Switzerland
