10. Your Rights Under Swiss Law

Under the Swiss Federal Data Protection Act (FADP), you have the following rights:

10.1 Right of Access

Request confirmation of what personal data we hold and receive a copy.

10.2 Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

10.3 Right to Erasure

Request deletion of personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.

10.4 Right to Data Portability

Receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.

10.5 Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

10.6 Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

10.7 Right to Lodge a Complaint

File a complaint with the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC):
Website: www.edoeb.admin.ch

How to Exercise Your Rights: Contact us at me@eflury.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days.
